# Drinks, snacks, and good conversation across disciplines

The AI4Science Club Zurich is hosting an informal social to bring our community together outside the usual talk format. Whether you work on machine learning, chemistry, biology, physics, or any other corner of the natural sciences, this is your chance to meet people from neighbouring fields, swap research stories, and find your next collaborator over a drink.

We'll provide drinks and snacks — just bring yourself and an open mind. Drop by whenever fits your evening; there's no fixed program, no slides, and no agenda beyond meeting interesting people.

Looking forward to seeing you there!
