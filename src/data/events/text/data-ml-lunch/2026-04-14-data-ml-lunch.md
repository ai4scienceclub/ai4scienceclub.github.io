# Scaling Message Passing to Simulate Proteins with DFT Accuracy

A heartfelt thank you to Felix Pultar for his fascinating presentation, Scaling Message Passing to Simulate Proteins with DFT Accuracy, in our Data & ML Lunch Series. It was a real pleasure to welcome Felix back to ETH and hear him share insights from his work.

We were also very happy to see so many new faces in the audience. The large number of participants and strong interest made for a wonderful atmosphere and a very enjoyable discussion. 

Thank you to everyone who joined us, and especially to Felix for contributing such an inspiring talk to the series.
