# AI4Science Club Zurich

AI4Science Club Zurich is a community for people who use AI to solve real scientific challenges.

Website: https://www.ai4science.ch

## Who We Are

We bring together researchers, students, and practitioners across disciplines to exchange ideas, learn from each other, and build collaborations at the intersection of AI and science.

The club was founded on January 23, 2026 by Malte Franke, Stefan P. Schmid, and Shaun O'Hare.

## Mission

Our mission is to connect people across fields and foster a vibrant AI4Science community in Zurich.

## Vision

Our long-term vision is to make Zurich the place to be in Europe for AI4Science: a collaborative ecosystem where ideas move quickly between disciplines and turn into real scientific progress.

## What We Do

- Host events, talks, and community meetups
- Connect people across chemistry, life sciences, materials, climate, physics, and more
- Create opportunities to find collaborators, mentors, and future co-founders
- Share practical workflows and real-world AI4Science applications
